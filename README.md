# Roamie | AI-Powered Personal Travel Assistant 🌍✈️

**Roamie** is an intelligent, all-in-one travel companion designed to eliminate the stress of trip planning. Developed as part of the **Artificial Intelligence (SECJ3553)** course at UTM, Roamie leverages AI to create personalized, budget-friendly itineraries that adapt in real-time.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
Traditional trip planning is fragmented, requiring users to switch between multiple apps for maps, translation, and bookings. **Roamie** solves this by consolidating the entire travel experience into a single intelligent interface.

### The Problem
* **App Fatigue:** Users struggle with switching between 4–5 different apps during a trip.
* **Optimization Issues:** Difficulty in planning routes that save time and stay within budget.
* **Language Barriers:** Communication gaps when traveling overseas.
* **Rigid Plans:** Weather changes or crowds often ruin static itineraries.

### The Solution
A mobile application that acts as a 24/7 travel assistant, providing automated scheduling, real-time adjustments, and immersive local experiences.

---

## ✨ Key Features
* **Smart Itinerary Generator:** Generates optimized routes based on travel dates, budget, and personal interests.
* **All-in-One Booking:** Integrated ticket purchasing for attractions directly within the app.
* **AI Translation:** Real-time speech-to-speech and image-to-text (OCR) translation for seamless local communication.
* **Dynamic Re-planning:** Automatically adjusts your schedule based on live weather updates, closures, or crowd density.
* **Immersive Experience:** Provides AI-powered tour narration and curated background music playlists tailored to your current location.
* **Budget Tracker:** Real-time expense monitoring with suggestions for cheaper local alternatives.

---

## 🎨 Design Thinking Process

### 1. Empathize
Through user interviews, we identified that travelers feel "messy and stressed" by the sheer amount of choices.
* **User Focus:** Busy professionals and students with limited time.
* **Key Finding:** Users value "confidence" and "flexibility" over a rigid, pre-set plan.

### 2. Define
We translated user pain points into specific functional requirements:
* **The Need:** An adaptive assistant that doesn't just plan a day, but *re-plans* it when obstacles arise.
* **The Goal:** Reduce planning time by 80% while ensuring users discover "hidden gems" they might otherwise miss.
